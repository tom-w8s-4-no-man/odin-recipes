Cacio e Pepe alla Gnocchi

Description

A simple yet delicious recipe that's sure to satisfy every time.

Takes less than 20 minutes total!

Ingredients

  • 1 box of your favorite gnocchi
  • 1 box Kosher salt — coarse is okay
  • 2.5 tsps freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tbsps unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmasean Cheese
  • 1 med. shallot, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, add salt as desired.
  2. When a boil is reached, turn down to medium.
  3. Add gnochi, stir gently and cook 2-3 minutes, or until floating.
  4. Drain — make sure to keep a cup of pasta water!
  5. Heat butter in skillet over med-high heat. Cook the shallots until tender, then add Parmasean, pepper, and gnocchi. Gradually add pasta water until sauce is creamy.
  6. Salt as needed. Garnish with more Parm and parsley.
  7. Enjoy!
