Sweet Potato Hash

Description

A little sweet, a little vegetal, a lot of yum.

For this hash, you can use whatever vegetables you'd like.

Takes about half an hour.

Ingredients

  • Depending on serving size, anywhere from 2-6 sweet potatoes
  • 1 Red bell peper
  • One bag baby spinach
  • 1 Yellow onion
  • 1 bundle asparagus

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375*
  2. Wash and dice sweet potatoes.
  3. Put onto baking sheet; sprinkle with salt; put into oven.
  4. On a separate sheet, put asparagus and peper, also to be placed in oven.
  5. Dice and cook onions over medium-light heat.
  6. When potatoes look about done, add spinach to pan.
  7. Mix it all up and enjoy!
