Roasted Garlic & Bean Soup

Description

Hearty, delicious, and healthy!

Takes around an hour.

Ingredients

  • Chicken stock | Veg. Stock + "Chik'n" Buillon, for meatless
  • 2 bulbs fresh garlic
  • 1 can Small White beans
  • 1 can Cannellini beans
  • 1 whole white or yellow onion
  • 1 stalk fresh celery
  • 1 bag baby spinach
  • 2 carrots
  • Fresh rosemary, thyme, & sage

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400*
  2. Put garlic, still in bulb, in oven. Let sit for approx. 45 minutes.
  3. Dice onion, chop celery & carrots.
  4. Watch Survivor while the garlic roasts.
  5. In dutch oven, add veggies until tender â€” medium heat.
  6. Add small white beans when veggies are tender. Turn down heat.
  7. When garlic is roasted, peel out of skin. Should be pastey.
  8. Add to dutch oven.
  9. Pour stock into dutch oven. Throw in a bay leaf, let contents simmer.
  10. IMPORTANT: Go back to watching Survivor.
  11. After 10 mins., in a cheesecloth, add fresh herbs to the mix during simmer.
  12. Fin.
